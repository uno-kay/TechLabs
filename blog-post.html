GOVERNMENT ANALYTIC PROGRAM

Loredana Papp Design, Inspiration 2 Comments

Michael Ward, director of municipal services for the Government Analytics Program (GAP) at the Collins Center for Public Management at the University of Massachusetts Boston puts the mission this way: “We want to use data to fix stuff.” GAP focuses on helping cities become more data-driven. The GAP programme provides entry-level data and analytics services for smaller municipalities (population: 100,000 or less) throughout Massachusetts. GAP’s clients are taking simple steps, for example, coding their accounts, to better address common financial challenges like police overtime.
